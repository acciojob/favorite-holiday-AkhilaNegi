My Favorite Holiday Destination: Bali

Bali is a beautiful island in Indonesia that is famous for its beaches, temples, and rice paddies. It is a popular tourist destination and offers a perfect blend of culture, nature, and adventure.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Ubud Monkey Forest - a sanctuary for Balinese long-tailed monkeys located in the heart of Ubud
  2. Tanah Lot Temple - a Hindu temple that sits on a rock formation by the sea
  3. Tegallalang Rice Terrace - a scenic rice terrace located in Ubud
  4. Kelingking Beach - a hidden beach with a unique rock formation resembling a T-Rex located in Nusa Penida
  5. Mount Batur - an active volcano that offers a stunning sunrise hike

Must-Try Foods

  • Nasi Goreng - fried rice with various ingredients such as egg, chicken, and vegetables
  • Sate - skewered meat (usually chicken or beef) served with peanut sauce
  • Nasi Campur - a dish that consists of rice, vegetables, and meat or fish
  • Babi Guling - roasted suckling pig served with rice and spicy sauce
  • Gado-Gado - a salad of boiled vegetables with peanut sauce dressing
